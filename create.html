What do you need to create a course on StepUp?

You just need to register on the site and agree to the terms of its use. After that, you can create a course and select the desired rate. The course on creating courses on StepUp and the Knowledge Base will help you in creating a course.

How do open courses differ from private ones?

An open course is hosted on MineProff and anyone can get access to it, all courses fall into the general Stepik catalog and can be found in the search. A private course is a closed course on Stepik, access to which is determined by the author himself, inviting people to the course using a link or adding himself. Private courses are not displayed via a direct link, as well as in the StepUp catalog.

What is the best tariff to choose?

If your goal is to create an open course for everyone, you will not have individual support for students and academic limitations, or if you want to work for MineProff for free - use the Basic plan. If your goal is to work with a small group of students or if you are interested in viewing report cards, course reports, exams, limiting attempts, issuing certificates, then we recommend PRO.

Can I switch between tariffs?

Yes, at any time. But if you leave PRO for Basic after the end of your subscription, the PRO features will no longer work and you will not be able to use them.
